# Week 1: AI Hairstyle Try-On App (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** User uploads a selfie and selects 1 of 4 hairstyle presets.

**Output:** A side-by-side "before and after" image comparison.

## 2. The Magical Moment

Instant, realistic self-transformation ("OMG, that's me with bangs!").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

Hairstyles only. No outfits, no custom typed prompts, no save/share buttons, and no lookbooks.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

A fun "vibe rating" or a sassy stylist note delivered alongside the final image.
